About Bayu Seafood

Uncompromising Freshness, Unrivaled Taste

As a proud member of the prestigious MFJ GROUP SDN BHD restaurant family, Bayu Seafood redefines the art of seafood dining in the heart of Kuala Lumpur.

Located at the Cascade Parking Bukit Aman, framed by the lush foliage and peaceful waters of Tasik Perdana, we serve 100% Halal-certified premium seafood. Guests can choose their catches directly from our live displays, ensuring absolute freshness in every dish.

Whether hosting large family reunions, exclusive corporate affairs, or intimate romantic dinners, our spacious lakeside setting guarantees an unforgettable atmosphere.
